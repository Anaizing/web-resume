Career Development

Founder | CEO

Soggy Ink Games

To memorize information is difficult and studying for an exam can be a demanding and stressful experience. Indeed, we value and respect all higher ed students working hard to achieve their goals, and continue to build on their knowledge post graduating, we want to support them by giving them access to new systems & innovative ways to help them commit new information to semantic memory. And in order to do this we are building an interactive fully immersive 3D platform where you can learn to memorize anything you want by playing a game. This platform will facilitate memorization, learning how to learn, as well as giving higher ed students the opportunity to connect with their peers who's goals are in alignment, in a safe virtual space. The benefits for the next generation of doctors, nurses, engineers, etc are limitless.
